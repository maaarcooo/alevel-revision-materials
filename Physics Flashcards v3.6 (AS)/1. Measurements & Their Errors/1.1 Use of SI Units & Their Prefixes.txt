How many SI base units are there? | Seven (six required for AQA A-Level)
What is the SI base unit of mass? | Kilogram (kg)
What is the SI base unit of length? | Metre (m)
What is the SI base unit of time? | Second (s)
What is the SI base unit of electric current? | Ampere (A)
What is the SI base unit of temperature? | Kelvin (K)
What is the SI base unit of amount of substance? | Mole (mol)
What are derived units? | Units formed by combining SI base units, deduced from the defining equation of the quantity
Units formed by combining SI base units using the defining equation of a quantity — what are these called? | Derived units
How do you find the SI base units of a derived unit? | Substitute the base units into the defining equation of that quantity
What is the newton (N) in SI base units? | kg m s⁻²
Which defining equation is used to derive the base units of the newton? | F = ma

Which defining equation is used to derive the base units of the joule? | E = ½mv²
What is the pascal (Pa) in SI base units? | kg m⁻¹ s⁻²
Which defining equation is used to derive the base units of the pascal? | P = F / A
